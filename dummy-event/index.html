============ About ============
01 · About

A forum for finance leaders across the Bowmark portfolio

Who attends

Senior finance leaders

CFOs and finance directors from Bowmark-backed companies, joined by a small number of sector specialists.

What to expect

Practical, peer-led discussion

Panels, short presentations and workshop-style sessions, combined with structured discussion. The focus is on sharing practical examples and creating space for peer exchange.

Why this year

From strategy to implementation

Focus is shifting from planning to delivery – strengthening pricing, cash performance and integration, alongside the targeted use of new tools.
