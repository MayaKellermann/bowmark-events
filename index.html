Activities

Refugio de Juanar hike

Route Description

A circular hike starting at Refugio de Juanar, passing through pine forest before opening into more exposed, rocky terrain.

The route offers panoramic views across the Costa del Sol and loops around the foothills of La Concha, returning via a well-marked descent.

Location

Marbella, Spain

Difficulty

Moderate

Distance

6.8 km

Elevation Gain

323 m

Duration

4 hours

Elevation Profile

Elevation profile: gradual start, followed by a moderate climb to c.1,120 m, then a steady descent.

Safety tips

  • Wear sturdy, comfortable shoes
  • Bring 1.5–2 litres of water
  • Use sun protection
  • Stay with your assigned group
  • Take care on uneven terrain

Facilities: No toilets along the route.
